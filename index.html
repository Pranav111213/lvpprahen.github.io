Roles/Responsibilities of SMC_TWR_TSO during LVP(SSI 01_2018 / MATS 2)

*CLD shall be manned during LVP

When any aircraft calls SMC for push back/start up during LVP, SMC shall

i. Pass the latest RVR information and trend.
ii. If the RVR is less than 550 M, check aircraft intention.
iii. If aircraft is ready, pushback/start up shall be approved according to sequence and traffic, else decide the sequence and intimate aircraft accordingly. In such cases aircraft will continue monitoring GROUND frequency.
iv. When Visibility/RVR permits, approve push back/start up as per the sequence decided earlier (refer pt. iii).
v. Clear departing aircraft to CAT-II/CAT-III holding point only.
vi. Approve taxi routings via LVP taxi routes.
vii. Use ITH judiciously.
viii. Handover one aircraft at a time to Tower Controller.
ix. SMC shall inform all taxiing aircraft of the preceding taxiing or holding aircraft.
x. Simultaneous push & start during LVP shall be suspended.


Tower Controller shall:

i. Use stop bars judiciously
ii. Approve taxi routings via LVP taxi routes.
iii. Landing clearance shall not be issued until preceding landing aircraft has vacated Localizer Sensitive Area (LSA) and confirmed by Pilot or preceding departing aircraft is airborne and has passed over the localizer antenna(in any case not later than 2 NM)
iv.Check ILS status
v.Check AGL lighting is correctly selected and operating properly
vi.Inform changes in RVR readings to the landing aircraft.
vi.Check transmissometers display


TSO shall:

i. Monitor selection of appropriate AGL including Stop bars in coordination with Tower and SMC Controllers.
ii. Monitor traffic on LVP taxiways, especially the hot spots.
iii. Prepare and mail LVP report (from 15 Nov to 15 Feb daily including NIL report and from 16 Feb to 14 Nov as and when LVP is implemented_check TLI 63/2019 for email addresses).
iv. Inform Aerodrome Rescue & Fire Fighting Services & AOCC
v.Check ATIS broadcast and include the message that “ILS CAT II/CAT III LOW VISIBILITY PROCEDURES IN FORCE”.
vi.TSO shall promptly inform SSO and/or AOCC, as appropriate, of any un-serviceability of ILS/ AGL observed or reported
vii.Check ASMGCS status (for operations below RVR 350 m)
ix.After implementation of LVP aeronautical ground lights must be inspected after every subsequent 02 hour period.
